Our commitment: We only use text messaging to communicate about your service experience (appointments, repair orders, updates, approvals, and pickup). We do not sell, share, rent, or disclose your information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Business Identity

Business: AAMCO Springdale (Automotive Repair & Service)
Location: Springdale, Arkansas
Purpose of Messaging: Customer care and service-related notifications

Questions? Contact us using the phone number or contact form listed on our website.

What Data We Collect

To provide service-related texting, we may collect and store:

  • Mobile phone number (for SMS communications)
  • Name (to personalize and correctly route communications)
  • Vehicle information (e.g., make/model/year/VIN, license plate if provided)
  • Service/repair order details (appointment times, estimates, approvals, status updates)
  • Message content and metadata (messages you send/receive, timestamps, and delivery status)

How We Use This Data

We use collected information solely to support your automotive service experience, including:

  • Confirming or reminding you of appointments
  • Providing repair status updates and completion notifications
  • Requesting or confirming estimate approvals
  • Coordinating parts availability and service timing
  • Responding to your questions about your vehicle or repair order
  • Operational needs such as recordkeeping, dispute resolution, and service quality

We do not use your phone number or texting data for advertising, lead resale, or marketing campaigns.

How We Obtain Consent

We only text customers who have provided their number for service communications and have consented to receive texts, for example by:

  • Providing your mobile number during scheduling or check-in and agreeing to receive service updates by text
  • Requesting text updates from our staff
  • Initiating a text message conversation with us

Message frequency varies based on your service needs. Message and data rates may apply.

Opt-Out and Help

  • Opt-out: Reply STOP to any message to stop receiving texts.
  • Help: Reply HELP for assistance.

After you opt out, you may receive a final confirmation message and we will stop sending SMS unless you opt back in.

Information Sharing

We do not share customer information with third parties for marketing or promotional purposes. We do not sell or rent your personal data.

We may use service providers strictly to deliver operational communications (for example, an SMS delivery platform). These providers are permitted to process information only to provide services to us and are not authorized to use it for their own marketing.

Data Retention

We retain customer and messaging records only as long as necessary for service operations, legal/compliance requirements, resolving disputes, and maintaining accurate business records.

Security

We take reasonable administrative, technical, and physical measures to protect customer information against unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
