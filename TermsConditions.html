AAMCO Springdale Service Updates Text Messaging Program

1. Program Name

AAMCO Springdale Service Updates Program

2. Program Description

The AAMCO Springdale Service Updates Program provides customers with service-related text messages regarding their automotive repair experience. Messages may include appointment confirmations, repair status updates, estimate approvals, parts notifications, vehicle pickup alerts, and other operational communications related to your service visit.

This program is intended strictly for customer care and service communication purposes and is not used for promotional marketing.

3. Message Frequency

Message frequency varies depending on your service activity and repair order status. You may receive multiple messages per service visit as needed to coordinate scheduling, approvals, and completion updates.

4. Message & Data Rates

Standard message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and wireless plan. AAMCO Springdale is not responsible for carrier-imposed charges.

5. Opt-Out Instructions

You may opt out of receiving SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message.

After you send STOP, you will receive a confirmation message and no further SMS messages will be sent unless you opt back in.
